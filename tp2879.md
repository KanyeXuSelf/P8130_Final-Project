### Import and Clean the Data

``` r
writing = read.csv(file = "./data/Project_1_data.csv") |> 
  janitor::clean_names() |> 
  mutate(across(where(is.character), ~na_if(.x, "")))
  
writing = writing |> 
  filter(!is.na(writing_score))
head(writing)
```
